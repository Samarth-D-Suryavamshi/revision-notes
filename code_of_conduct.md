# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to fostering an open, respectful, and collaborative environment for everyone participating in this project.

Contributors, maintainers, and community members are expected to interact professionally and respectfully regardless of experience level, background, or identity.

---

## Expected Behavior

Examples of positive behavior include:

* Being respectful and constructive.
* Providing helpful technical feedback.
* Accepting constructive criticism professionally.
* Focusing discussions on technical improvements.
* Respecting different viewpoints and approaches.
* Helping maintain a welcoming learning environment.

---

## Unacceptable Behavior

The following behaviors are not tolerated:

* Harassment or personal attacks.
* Discrimination of any kind.
* Offensive or abusive language.
* Trolling or deliberately disruptive behavior.
* Publishing another person's private information without permission.
* Spam, self-promotion, or unrelated advertising.
* Knowingly submitting misleading or malicious contributions.

---

## Contribution Standards

Contributors should strive to:

* Write technically accurate documentation.
* Verify information before submitting changes.
* Follow the repository structure and contribution guidelines.
* Maintain clear, concise, and professional writing.
* Submit production-quality examples where applicable.

---

## Enforcement

Project maintainers reserve the right to edit, reject, or remove contributions that violate this Code of Conduct.

Repeated violations may result in temporary or permanent removal from participation in the project.

---

## Reporting Issues

If you encounter behavior that violates this Code of Conduct, please open a private discussion or contact the repository maintainer through GitHub.

All reports will be reviewed fairly and handled confidentially whenever possible.

---

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to:

* GitHub Issues
* Pull Requests
* Discussions
* Documentation contributions
* Project-related communication

---
